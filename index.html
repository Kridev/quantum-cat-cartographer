QUANTUM CAT CARTOGRAPHER

Six realities. Cats drift in. Each cat has a hidden preferred reality (matching its node's color). Place them right, keep stability above zero, survive escalating waves.

Controls

  • Click a solid cat to select it — connected nodes light up
  • Click a glowing node to move it (only adjacent nodes are reachable)
  • Click a faded Schrödinger cat to observe it — collapses to that node
  • Esc deselects

Schrödinger cats

  • Appear faded and pulsing on 2–3 nodes simultaneously — same cat, multiple places
  • Don't count toward node capacity until observed
  • Clicking one of its copies collapses the waveform — it becomes solid at the node you clicked
  • You choose the collapse location: pick wisely (near its preferred reality)

Scoring & stability

  • +5 for observing a Schrödinger cat
  • +10 when you place a cat in its preferred reality
  • Each node has a capacity. Exceeding it drains stability (−2/sec per excess cat)
  • Stability slowly regenerates when no node is overloaded
  • Stability hits 0 → reality collapses → game over
⬤ correctly placed (vanishes) ⬤ superposition ⬤ overloaded
